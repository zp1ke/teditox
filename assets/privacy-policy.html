Privacy Policy for Teditox

Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 12, 2025

Teditox ("we", "our", "us") is a text editor app designed to help you edit text files on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use Teditox.

Data Collection and Usage

Teditox does not require user authentication and does not collect any personal information. All data related to your edited files, app settings, and recent files list are stored locally on your device.

Local Data Storage

  • Edited Files:  The text files you edit are stored locally on your device in your chosen locations.

  • App Settings:  Your app settings (e.g., theme, font size) are stored locally to personalize your editing experience.

  • Recent Files List:  A list of recently opened files is stored locally for quick access.

Data Sharing

Teditox does not share your data with any third parties. All data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or services, unless you explicitly choose to share files using your device's sharing functionalities.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security measures. We recommend using a secure password or biometric authentication to protect your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
